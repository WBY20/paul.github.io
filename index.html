I am a PhD student at Automation, Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Shao Li.

My research interest lies in Bioinformatics, AI in TCM, AI in Biomedicine and CRISPR system design.

My specific research areas include novel drug development based on bioinformatics analysis, as well as association prediction and protein design using network models, MLP, GNNs, and LLMs.
